Privacy Policy

Your Privacy Matters

At Shine, we respect your privacy. Our apps collect only the data necessary to provide core functionality. We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties.

Data Collection and Usage

We believe in privacy by design. The data collected is limited to **non-personally identifiable usage statistics** necessary for improving app performance and user experience. This includes information like device type, crash reports, and basic feature interaction, but **never** your name, email, or precise location.

Third-Party Services

We utilize minimal, privacy-focused third-party services (such as Google Play Services for distribution) which operate under their own strict privacy policies. We ensure that data shared with these services is minimized and anonymized wherever possible.

Policy Changes

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by updating the policy within the app or on this page. Your continued use of the app after such updates constitutes acceptance of the new terms.
